Salmon Recipe

Description

This salmon is to die for.
It is literally the yummiest salmon I know you've ever had!

Ingredients

  • Olive Oil
  • Salmon
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Smoked Paprika
  • Salmon Rub
  • Lawry's Seasoned Salt

Steps

  1. Rub the olive oil on your salmon
  2. Sprinkle some salt
  3. Put on that pepper
  4. It's time for the smoked paprika
  5. How about adding a little salmon rub?
  6. Next, it's time for that Lawry's seasoned salt
  7. Preheat oven to 350 degree fahrenheit
  8. Cook for 12-15 minutes
  9. Enjoy!
