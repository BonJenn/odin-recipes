Cereal Recipe

Description

This cereal is seriously going to rock your world.
It's the most delicious breakfast treat.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups of rolled oats
  • 1 cup of nuts
  • 1 cup of drief fruits
  • 1/2 cup of honey
  • 1/4 cup of vegetable oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 teaspoon of cinammon

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 300 degrees F (150 degrees C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the oats, nuts, salt, and cinnamon.
  3. In a separate bowl, mix together the honey (or maple syrup), oil, and vanilla extract.
  4. Pour the wet ingredients into the oat mixture and stir until all the oats and nuts are thoroughly coated.
  5. Spread the mixture evenly on the prepared baking sheet.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven for about 25-30 minutes, or until the granola is golden brown. Stir every 10 minutes to ensure even baking.
  7. Let the granola cool completely on the baking sheet. It will become crisp as it cools.
  8. Once cooled, mix in the dried fruit.
  9. Store the granola in an airtight container at room temperature. It will stay fresh for about 2 weeks.
