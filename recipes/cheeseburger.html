Cheeseburger Recipe

Description

This is the juiciest cheeseburger you'll ever eat in your entire life.
It's hot and steamy, and full of delicious vegetables!

Ingredients

  • Hamburger Buns
  • Ground Meat
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Onions
  • Mustard
  • Mayonaise
  • Ketchup
  • Cheese
  • Butter
  • Pickles

Steps

  1. Head up your stove
  2. Mix the salt and pepper into the meat
  3. Place the meat on the stove
  4. Apply butter to the bottom of each bun and toast it on the stove
  5. Once the meat starts bleeding at the top, flip it over
  6. Add your cheese
  7. Chop up your vegetables
  8. Add your meat and vegetables to the burger
  9. In a bowl, mix the ketchup, mayonaise, and mustard together and apply it to the burger
  10. Enjoy!
