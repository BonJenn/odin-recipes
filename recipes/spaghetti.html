Spaghetti Recipe

Description

This is the juiciest cheeseburger you'll ever eat in your entire life.
It's hot and steamy, and full of delicious vegetables!

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, finely diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 can, 28 ounces of crushed tomatos
  • 1 teaspoon of dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • pinch of sugar
  • 1 pound of spaghetti
  • salt for pasta and water

Steps

  1. Start the sauceHeat the olive oil in a large saucepan over medium heat. Add the diced onion and cook until it becomes translucent, about 5 minutes. Add the minced garlic and cook for another minute until fragrant.
  2. Add the tomatoes and spicesPour in the crushed tomatoes, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper. If your tomatoes are a bit acidic, a pinch of sugar can help balance the flavors. Stir everything together and let the sauce come to a simmer.
  3. Let the sauce cookReduce the heat to low and let the sauce simmer for about 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.
  4. Cook the spaghettiWhile the sauce is simmering, fill a large pot with water, add a generous amount of salt, and bring it to a boil. Add the spaghetti and cook according to the package instructions until al dente.
  5. Drain the spaghettiOnce the spaghetti is cooked, drain it in a colander. Do not rinse the spaghetti as the starch helps the sauce adhere better.
  6. Combine the spaghetti and sauceAdd the drained spaghetti to the saucepan with the sauce. Toss until the spaghetti is fully coated in the sauce.
  7. Serve hot with a sprinkling of freshly grated Parmesan cheese and a side of garlic bread, if desired. Enjoy your homemade spaghetti with marinara sauce!
